Schmucker Art Gallery, Gettysburg College

Making Her Mark

Fifty Women Artists of the Historic Woodstock Art Colony

January 28 - April 11, 2026

This exhibition celebrates the remarkable women who shaped one of America's most storied artist colonies. Working in Woodstock, New York from the early twentieth century onward, these painters, printmakers, and sculptors forged careers outside the mainstream art world.
